Our Story

Made with Love,
Rooted in Africa

Mama Africa Meals was born from a simple idea — nobody in Madison was cooking the food we grew up with. The jollof rice that tastes like home. The shawarma with the right sauce. The porridge that warms you on a cold Wisconsin night.

Every meal is made fresh in a real kitchen, with real ingredients, the way our families taught us. No shortcuts. No compromises.

Based in Madison, WI — serving the whole community, one plate at a time.
